NHL’s 4 Nations Faceoff shows USA is catching up to Canada as the world’s best hockey nation

By Will McLernon

Team USA sent a message during NHL’s 4 Nation’s Faceoff that they are closing in on Canada as the world’s top hockey country.

The Americans beat their northern rivals in the group stage in a thrilling 3-1 victory which featured three fights in the first nine seconds, but fell just short of hoisting the 4 Nations trophy in Boston after Canada’s Connor McDavid sniped a shot from the slot past Team USA goalie Connor Hellebuyck in overtime.

Will Mclernon is a Master's Student at Northwestern University's Medill School of Journalism.
